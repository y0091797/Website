The Magic Forest

3D Artist · Modelling · Rigging · Animation

Freya overcomes her grief with the help of a friend and her endless imagination.

The Magic Forest is a self-written story about Freya, a little girl who finds herself lost in the woods. At first her sadness and helplessness feel endless in a forever-ongoing, dark and deep forest. Luckily, with the help of a strangely familiar firefly, she learns to see the light and colourfulness even in the darkest times.
