My name is Moritz, I'm from Germany and studied information science before discovering my love for 3D modelling and animation during my master's in Media & Game Development at Hochschule Harz.

In my free time I give free rein to my creativity, designing and modelling 3D characters with unique features that I later bring to life through animation. As a game-development student I also love playing video games — especially multiplayer action and role-play titles for their variety of characters and the chance to laugh (and rage) with friends.

When I've had enough screen time, I clear my head swimming or bouldering. Beyond my professional skills, I value my social side — my empathy and need for harmony help me build friendships across very different people, which has always benefited my studies and work.
