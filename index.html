My name is Moritz, I am from Germany and successfully studied information science even though I rapidly discovered a kind of antipathy for programming. That´s why I am currently discovering my creativity and love for 3D-Modelling and Animation during my masters in "Media and Gameconcipation" at the Hochschule Harz.
During my free time, I love to give free rein to my creativity while designing and modeling 3D characters. As I give my characters unique features I can later bring them to life during the animation.
As a student of gameconcipation, I also enjoy playing video games most of the time. Mostly I am a sucker for multiplayer action and roleplay games, simply because they offer the most variety of different characters and also give me the ability to stay in contact with my friends to have a laugh and also some rage. Whenever I have spent enough time in front of my PC I like to exercise and get my head off while going for a swim or going boulders.
To my strengths apart from my professional competence in information science I account for my social skills. Thanks to my sympathy and my need for harmony I always make friends with a large variety of people which till now always had benefits for my studying and working environment.
