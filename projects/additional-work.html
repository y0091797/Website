Additional Work

A small collection from the workshop: a finished animated short film and a fully rendered interior scene. Different briefs, same obsession with light, staging and detail.

The Sorcerer's Apprentice

A 3D motion-capture animated short film. Faelivrin wants to be as talented as his wizarding master — but conjuring a demon on his own goes horribly wrong, and he's left trapped in a fragile circle of safety.

I handled the characters, environment, rigging and animation, writing the story and staging every shot of the film.

3D Children's Room

A highly detailed interior modelled and rendered in Blender Cycles — a full hour of render time per frame. An exercise in props, materials and warm, believable lighting.

  • Every prop modelled from scratch — bed, wardrobe, slide, lamps
  • Cycles lighting tuned for a soft, lived-in feel
  • 4K final renders, front and side views
