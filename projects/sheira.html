Sheira

Sheira is a character concept built around the contrast between elegance and danger. By day, she is a celebrated opera singer. Hidden beneath the spotlight, however, is a siren whose voice has the power to enchant, manipulate and destroy.

Discipline
Character Design · 3D Modeling · Story Telling

The project began as a narrative character concept rather than a 3D model. Through worldbuilding, story development and character exploration, I developed Sheira's background, motivations and visual identity before translating the concept into a stylized character design.
